Carrot Hot Dogs

This is a carrot. You want straighter ones than this

Description:

A vegetarian alternative to a hot dog, without all the additives and mystery meat

Ingredients:

  • 8 medium carrots peeled and skinny ends trimmed. You want straight, thick ones to match hot dog shape
  • 2 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 c. low sodium vegetable broth. If you can't find low sodium, it will taste better but be less healthy.
  • 1/4 c. apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tsp. maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. smoked paprika. This is a spice - do not smoke paprika
  • 1/2tsp. liquid smoke
  • 8 hot dog buns

Steps:

  1. Add the carrots to small roasting pan and toss with olive oil. In a small bowl, wkisk together broth, vinegar, soy sauce, maple syrup, garlic powder, paprika and liquid smoke. Pour over carrots and cover pan wiht foil. Let sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Place the foil-covered roasting pan in the oven and roast carrots for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and roast for another 20 minutes, until the carrots are just tender enough to stab with a fork.
  3. Serve carrots on hot dog buns with condiments of your choice.
